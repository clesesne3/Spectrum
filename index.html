What is SPECTRUM?

Why the need?

SPECTRUM is focused on increasing the presence of traditionally underrepresented individuals in the fields of science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM). We create innovative ways to establish academic and professional pipelines into STEM careers through educational guidance, scholarship funding, and community engagement.

Diversity in STEM is necessary for innovation in science and technology. While we have seen improvement in the recruitment and retention of students and professionals of color, there is considerable room for growth. Improving access to resources that serve as stepping blocks toward successful careers in STEM fields is paramount in addressing our lack of representation!
